# Vision

## Overview

LifeOverYears is an AI-powered platform for reconstructing historically inspired versions of modern locations.

The platform transforms present-day photographs into immersive visual representations of the past by combining structured scene understanding, historical knowledge, and generative AI.

Unlike traditional image generation systems, LifeOverYears is built around a reusable knowledge model rather than prompts alone. Every generated scene is based on a structured representation called **SceneDNA**, allowing consistent, repeatable, and scalable reconstruction across different years, regions, and brands.

The project is designed as an automated content production pipeline capable of generating high-quality historical visualizations and publishing them across multiple social media platforms.

## Problem Statement

Every day thousands of recognizable places disappear. Most people remember ordinary places rather than famous landmarks. LifeOverYears preserves those memories by reconstructing historically inspired versions of modern locations.

## Vision

Create the world's largest digital reconstruction platform for everyday American history.

## Guiding Principles

- Knowledge First
- Provider Independence
- Consistency
- Scalability
- Human Supervision

## Long-Term Goals

- Build a reusable historical knowledge base.
- Automate historical scene reconstruction.
- Generate multi-platform content.
- Preserve everyday American memories.

## Non Goals

- Not a forensic reconstruction tool.
- Not intended to reproduce copyrighted photographs.
- Not tied to any AI provider.
- Not focused on perfect historical accuracy.

## Success Criteria

A single modern photograph can be transformed into a historically inspired, emotionally engaging video through an automated pipeline with minimal human intervention.

# EraProfile

## Purpose

EraProfile represents the historical characteristics of a specific period.

It describes how the world looked during that period.

EraProfile does not describe a specific location.

EraProfile provides historical context that can be applied to any Scene.

---

# Philosophy

SceneDNA defines physical reality.

EraProfile defines historical reality.

The combination of both allows the reconstruction of the past.

SceneDNA answers:

"What exists?"

EraProfile answers:

"What existed during this period?"

---

# Era Identity

Examples:

* 1940
* 1955
* 1968
* 1975
* 1984
* 1992
* 2001

An EraProfile may represent:

* a year
* a range of years
* a cultural period
* a regional historical period

# Relationship to SceneDNA

SceneDNA and EraProfile are independent.

SceneDNA contains permanent physical information.

EraProfile contains temporary historical information.

The same SceneDNA may be reconstructed using multiple EraProfiles.

Examples:

* SceneDNA + 1955
* SceneDNA + 1975
* SceneDNA + 1995
* SceneDNA + 2010

---

# Relationship to Knowledge

EraProfile does not contain raw historical sources.

Instead, it references historical knowledge.

Examples:

* historical vehicle databases
* business directories
* architectural references
* advertising archives
* historical photographs
* local records

Knowledge may evolve.

EraProfile remains stable.

---

# Relationship to Prompt

EraProfile is one of the primary inputs used for Prompt generation.

Prompt combines:

* SceneDNA
* EraProfile
* Knowledge
* Style
* Generation settings

EraProfile itself never contains prompts.

---

# Design Principles

* EraProfile is independent of SceneDNA.
* EraProfile is independent of AI providers.
* EraProfile contains no prompts.
* EraProfile contains no generated images.
* EraProfile describes historical context.
* Multiple Scenes may use the same EraProfile.
* EraProfile may be expanded without changing the Domain Model.

---

# Summary

EraProfile represents the historical characteristics of a specific period.

It separates historical reality from physical reality.

Together with SceneDNA, it forms the foundation of historical reconstruction in the LifeOverYears platform.
